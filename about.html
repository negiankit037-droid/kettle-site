Our Story

The Kumaon Kettle is not just a cafe; it's a blend of Kumaoni Tradition and Modern Comfort. Located in the serene hills of Ghorakhal, we bring you the authentic taste of the mountains with a touch of contemporary flair.


Whether it's our specialty ginger tea or our premium dining menu, every dish tells a story of the hills. Come, sit back, and enjoy the view with a perfect kettle of tea.
